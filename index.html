Starfish Games

Starfish Games is a one-man brand devoted to the creation of tabletop boardgames, storytelling games and roleplaying games. We focus on bizarre and new game mechanics or themes.

  1. Shoulda, woulda, coulda - a card-based game on fantasy guilt trips among tired adventurers in a shady brewery. Read more!
  2. Nightshade - A solo tabletop boardgame that allows players to create noir story plots. Grab your dice and get ready to investigate! Try it now!
  3. Whispering Willows - A "Powered by the Apocalypse" post-apocalyptic fantasy roleplaying game on anthropomorphic animals whose world has been turned upside-down by an evil entity. Still in progress.
  4. Dark Shroud - In a far-away future, humanity has changed. Now, different breeds of human-like creatures dwell on Earth and fight to keep the balance. Try it now!
  5. The Wild Continent of Ca Nin - In the wild continent of Ca Nin, things are getting pretty shaky for the animal folks who inhabit it. A world-building game for 2-6 players. Try it now!
